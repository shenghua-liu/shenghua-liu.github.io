Shenghua Liu is a full Professor at the Institute of Computing
Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. He received his Ph.D. degree from the
Computer Science and Technology Department, Tsinghua University. He once
visited at the University of California, Los Angeles and a visiting scholar at
Carnegie Mellon University respectively. His current research interests are
designing intelligent and automated algorithms for big data mining problems,
related to big graphs and series. Two related publications have been recognized as
``best paper'' award and candidate.
